# CCVLD

Phan mem ho tro VLD — tai ban moi nhat va chay `CCVLD.exe`.

## Tai ve (khuyen dung)

**[Releases — tai CCVLD.exe hoac CCVLD.zip](https://github.com/3122380192/CCVLD/releases/latest)**

Hoac zip truc tiep: [CCVLD.zip](https://github.com/3122380192/CCVLD/raw/main/CCVLD.zip)

## Cai dat

1. Tai **CCVLD.zip** tu Releases (khuyen dung — day du file)
2. Giai nen vao `C:\CCVLD` (hoac bat ky thu muc)
3. Chay `CCVLD.exe` — shortcut **CCVLD** tren Desktop se duoc tao khi cap nhat tu app

Neu chi tai `CCVLD.exe`, giai nen cung thu muc voi `libgcc_s_dw2-1.dll` va `libstdc++-6.dll` (co trong Release).

## Cap nhat tu app (PRO / VIP)

- Khi mo GUI, app tu kiem tra [version.txt](https://github.com/3122380192/CCVLD/raw/main/version.txt)
- Co ban moi → tai zip (GUI + anh BMP + VLD.dll + move.txt) ve `C:\CCVLD`
- Tao shortcut **CCVLD** tren Desktop va khoi dong lai
- Cap **FREE** khong duoc tu dong cap nhat
